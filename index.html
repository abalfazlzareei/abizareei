Abalfazl Zareei

Researcher @SBS SU, @SHOF

Email: abalfazl.zareei@sbs.su.se
abalfazl.zareei@gmail.com

I am currently assistant professor in finance in Stockholm Business School at Stockholm University and Visiting Research Fellow at Swedish House of Finance.

I received my Ph.D. in Finance from the Carlos III de Madrid University. My Ph.D. research was focused on the application of network theory in the portfolio selection process. My research interests are empirical asset pricing, machine learning and network analysis.
